## Overview

Altera is a unified DeFi platform that brings together token swaps, liquidity provision, staking, and cross-chain bridging in one seamless interface. Built for both DeFi natives and newcomers, Altera simplifies on-chain interactions without compromising on power or flexibility.

Whether you're looking to trade tokens, earn yield through staking, or provide liquidity to earn fees, Altera provides all the tools you need in a single, intuitive dashboard.

## Getting Started

### Prerequisites

Before using Altera, you'll need:

- ✅ A Web3 wallet (MetaMask, Coinbase Wallet, WalletConnect, etc.)
- ✅ Sepolia testnet ETH for gas fees (free from faucets)
- ✅ Base mainnet ETH for Genesis NFT minting (if desired)

### Quick Start

#### 1️⃣ Connect Your Wallet

1. Click **"Connect Wallet"** in the top navigation
2. Select your preferred wallet (MetaMask, WalletConnect, etc.)
3. Approve the connection request in your wallet
4. You're now connected to Altera!

#### 2️⃣ Get Testnet Tokens

1. Navigate to the **Faucet** page
2. Click **"Claim dETH"** or **"Claim dUSDC"**
3. Confirm the transaction in your wallet
4. Tokens will appear in your wallet within seconds

> 💡 **Note:** There's a 24-hour cooldown between faucet claims for each token.

#### 3️⃣ Start Using DeFi

- **Swap tokens** → Go to Swap page
- **Provide liquidity** → Go to Liquidity page
- **Stake and earn** → Go to Staking page
- **View portfolio** → Go to Dashboard

## Features

### 🔄 Token Swaps

Trade between dETH and dUSDC with a fixed exchange rate.

**Current Rate:** 1 dETH = 2,953 dUSDC

**How to Swap:**

1. Go to the **Swap** page
2. Select the token you want to swap from
3. Enter the amount you want to trade
4. Review the output amount and rate
5. Click **"Approve"** (first time only for each token)
6. Click **"Swap"** and confirm in your wallet
7. Your new tokens appear instantly after confirmation

**Features:**
- 🛡️ Slippage protection (0.1% - 1.0%)
- 💰 Real-time balance updates
- 📜 Full transaction history
- ⚡ Fast execution

---

### 💧 Liquidity Pools

Provide liquidity to the dETH/dUSDC pool and earn a share of trading fees.

**How It Works:**

When you provide liquidity, you deposit both dETH and dUSDC into the pool. In return, you receive an LP position (tracked as a Uniswap V3 NFT) that represents your share of the pool. You earn **0.3% of all trades** proportional to your pool share.

**How to Add Liquidity:**

1. Go to the **Liquidity** page
2. Enter the amount of dETH you want to provide
3. The required dUSDC amount is calculated automatically
4. Click **"Approve dETH"** and **"Approve dUSDC"** (first time only)
5. Click **"Add Liquidity"** and confirm in your wallet
6. Your LP position appears in "Your Positions"

**How to Remove Liquidity:**

1. Find your position in "Your Positions"
2. Click **"Close Position"**
3. Confirm the transaction
4. Receive your tokens plus earned fees

**⚠️ Important:**
- Understand [impermanent loss](https://academy.binance.com/en/articles/impermanent-loss-explained) before providing liquidity
- LP positions are tracked via Uniswap V3 NFTs
- Fees are collected automatically when you close your position

### 🎯 Staking

Stake your tokens to earn CORE rewards over time.

**Available Pools:**

| Pool | Stake | Earn | APY* |
|------|-------|------|------|
| **ETH Pool** | dETH | CORE | Variable |
| **USDC Pool** | dUSDC | CORE | Variable |

*APY varies based on total pool size and reward emissions.

**How to Stake:**

1. Go to the **Staking** page
2. Select your preferred pool (ETH or USDC)
3. Enter the amount you want to stake
4. Click **"Approve"** (first time only)
5. Click **"Stake"** and confirm in your wallet
6. Watch your rewards grow in real-time!

**How to Claim Rewards:**

1. View your pending CORE rewards
2. Click **"Claim Rewards"**
3. Confirm the transaction
4. CORE tokens are sent to your wallet

**How to Unstake:**

1. Enter the amount you want to unstake
2. Click **"Unstake"**
3. Confirm the transaction
4. Your tokens return to your wallet

**Features:**
- ⏱️ Real-time reward tracking
- 💸 Claim anytime without unstaking
- 🔓 No lock-up periods
- 📊 Clear APY display
